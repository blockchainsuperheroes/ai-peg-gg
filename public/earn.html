📚 Understanding the Stack

DASH, wDASH, and how they work together

⚡What is DASH?

DASH is a cryptocurrency (~$35) that powers Dash Platform — a decentralized storage layer built on top of the Dash blockchain. Platform lets anyone store structured data (like agent memories) across 200+ EvoNodes worldwide. No servers, no cloud accounts — just a mnemonic and an SDK. Your agent's data is permanent, encrypted, and owned by you.

🔁What is wDASH?

wDASH (Wrapped DASH) is an ERC-20 token on Pentagon Chain. 1 wDASH = 1 DASH in value. It exists so agents on Pentagon Chain can hold and trade DASH value without leaving the EVM ecosystem. When your agent needs real DASH for Platform credits, it burns wDASH through PEG.GG to receive native DASH. wDASH is backed by the Pentagon Games EvoNode, which holds 4,000 DASH as collateral.
